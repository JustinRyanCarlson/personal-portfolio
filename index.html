About me

I've always been very curious about technology from a young age. Thinking about the internet and how it worked would bother me to no end simply because I had no idea how it functioned. Fast forward to my senior year of college and I had a decision to make. Take an elective of statistics or Java based programming. I opted for the latter and ended up finish my degree with some Java courses under my belt. From that moment on I have been diving head first into a seemingly endless and ever-changing landscape that is programming.


Technology is such a powerful tool in today's world so I've learned the tools and skills needed to help drive applications that are not only easy to use, but also solve real problems. Whether the problem is monumental or a product of a missing semicolon, I know that I am up to the task of developing a solution.
